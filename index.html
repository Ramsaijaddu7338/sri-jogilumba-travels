Our Own Bus Garage

Complete In-House Maintenance & Repairs for Maximum Reliability

Complete In-House Maintenance Facility

Sri Jogulamba Travels operates its own fully-equipped bus garage, ensuring complete control over vehicle maintenance and quality.

Our state-of-the-art facility handles all types of work in-house, from routine servicing to major repairs, guaranteeing that every vehicle in our fleet meets the highest standards of safety and reliability.

Key Advantages:
Complete Mechanical Repairs

Full engine work and mechanical repairs performed by certified technicians

Body Repairs & Painting

Complete bodywork and professional painting services

Electrical & AC Repairs

Expert electrical systems and air conditioning maintenance

Our Comprehensive Garage Services

Mechanical Repairs

Complete engine diagnostics, repairs, and overhaul services by certified mechanics. We handle everything from minor tune-ups to major engine rebuilds.

Body Repairs & Painting

Professional dent removal, panel beating, and high-quality painting services. We restore vehicles to their original condition after accidents or wear.

Electrical Systems

Complete electrical diagnostics, wiring repairs, and component replacements. We handle all electrical systems including lighting, signals, and entertainment systems.

AC Repairs

Air conditioning system maintenance, gas charging, and component repairs. We ensure your comfort with fully functional AC systems in all seasons.

Wheel Alignment

Precision wheel alignment, balancing, and suspension system maintenance. We ensure smooth rides and even tire wear for optimal safety and comfort.

Tyre Services

Tyre change, rotation, balancing, and pressure monitoring for optimal safety. We use premium quality tires that are suitable for long-distance travel.

How Our Garage Benefits You

Enhanced Safety

Every vehicle undergoes strict safety checks and maintenance before each trip, ensuring maximum safety for passengers. Our in-house garage allows us to maintain higher safety standards.

Reduced Breakdowns

Preventive maintenance and regular servicing minimize unexpected issues on the road, ensuring smooth journeys. Our comprehensive checks catch potential problems before they occur.

Faster Turnaround

In-house repairs mean quicker service and minimal downtime for your booked vehicles. No waiting for external garages to complete repairs.

Cost Efficiency

Direct control over maintenance costs translates to better value and competitive pricing for our customers. We pass the savings from our efficient garage operations to you.
